Website Page Structure
1. Home Page

Page name: Home
Main heading:
Mars Is No Longer a Dream. It’s the Next Address of Humanity.

Use this page to introduce the full vision quickly.

Content to use here:

“Earth was the beginning. Mars is the future.”
“Mars is no longer viewed as science fiction.”
“This is bigger than real estate. This is history.”
“Secure your place in the future today.”

Purpose of page:
To attract visitors immediately and make them understand your website is about a futuristic Mars property/investment concept.

Button text ideas:

Learn About The Vision
Explore Mars Legacy Estates
Request Private Information
Join Early Access
2. About Us Page

Page name: About Mars Legacy Estates
Main heading:
Where The Future Begins

Use the Mars Legacy Estates section here.

Content to use here:

“Mars Legacy Estates is a conceptual ultra-premium interplanetary property opportunity inspired by the coming era of space civilization and multi-planetary living.”
“For the few who understand that true wealth is built not only through assets, but through positioning ahead of history itself.”
“Mars represents more than property. It represents legacy, prestige, innovation, and early positioning in humanity’s next era.”

Purpose of page:
Explain who you are and what your website/project represents.

Better wording:
Instead of saying you are selling actual Mars land directly, say:

Mars Legacy Estates is a future-focused concept built around the vision of interplanetary living, symbolic property positioning, and early participation in humanity’s next frontier.

3. Our Vision Page

Page name: Our Vision
Main heading:
Humanity’s Next Chapter Begins Beyond Earth

Use the first part about SpaceX’s Mars project and multiplanetary civilization.

Content to use here:

“The long-term goal is not just visiting Mars — it is building a permanent, self-sustaining human civilization there.”
“Humanity should become multiplanetary in case something catastrophic happens on Earth.”
“The real success metric is: Can Mars survive even if Earth can’t?”

Purpose of page:
Explain the big mission behind the project.

Sections under this page:

The Mars Vision
Becoming Multiplanetary
Why The Future Needs New Frontiers
Inspired By Space Innovation
4. Why Mars Page

Page name: Why Mars?
Main heading:
Why Mars Is The Next Frontier

Use the section that explains why Mars was chosen.

Content to use here:

Mars has water ice
Days are similar to Earth
It has gravity
It has an atmosphere
It has future settlement potential

Then include the hard reality:

Freezing temperatures
Radiation
Dust storms
Thin atmosphere
No breathable oxygen

Purpose of page:
Educate people about why Mars matters, but also show that Mars is difficult.

Better closing line:

Mars is not easy. That is exactly why it represents one of the greatest challenges and opportunities in human history.

5. Future Life On Mars Page

Page name: Life On Mars
Main heading:
Imagine The Year 2087

Use your storytelling section here.

This is the part that starts:

“The year is 2087. The first thing you notice on Mars is the silence.”

This page should feel emotional and cinematic.

Purpose of page:
Make visitors imagine what life on Mars could feel like.

Sections under this page:

The First Mars City
Children Born On Mars
Life Inside The Habitats
Cargo Ships From Earth
The Birth Of Martian Culture
Humanity Becomes A Two-Planet Species

This is one of your strongest pieces. Use it as a vision/story page, not as an investment page.

6. Why Live On Mars Page

Page name: Why Live On Mars?
Main heading:
The Case For Living Beyond Earth

Use your section titled “Why live in Mars?”

Fix the grammar to:

Why Live On Mars?

Content to use here:

Mars could be a backup for humanity
Mars could create scientific discoveries
Mars could produce new technologies
Mars could create new industries
Mars could unite humanity with a bigger purpose

Purpose of page:
Explain the human, scientific, and civilization reasons for Mars settlement.

Good quote to use:

“Humanity becomes stronger, safer, smarter, and more advanced when it learns how to live beyond Earth.”

7. Multiplanetary Civilization Page

Page name: Multiplanetary Future
Main heading:
The Benefits Of A Multiplanetary Civilization

Use your section about the benefits of humans living on more than one planet.

Fix the numbering, because your current text repeats number 1 many times.

Use this structure:

Survival Of Humanity
Scientific Discoveries
Faster Technology Progress
New Economic Opportunities
Human Exploration And Purpose
Better Understanding Of Earth
Long-Term Expansion Of Civilization
Cultural Evolution

Purpose of page:
This page should be educational and visionary.

8. Investment Opportunity Page

Page name: Investment Opportunity
Main heading:
Positioning Early In The Next Frontier

Use the sections:

“Why choose to buy properties on Mars?”
“Reason why you need to invest in Mars”
“Imagine owning property on Mars before the rest of humanity fully arrives.”

But make the wording safer.

Instead of:

“The first people who secure land or infrastructure rights there could become unbelievably wealthy”

Use:

“Those who position early around future Mars infrastructure, branding, research, and development concepts may gain strategic long-term advantages as interest in interplanetary living grows.”

Purpose of page:
Explain the investment idea without making dangerous promises.

9. Properties / Locations Page

Page name: Future Property Concepts
Main heading:
Strategic Locations For A Future Mars Civilization

Use the section about location:

Views of Olympus Mons
Underground radiation-safe zones
Proximity to transport hubs
Terraforming regions
Luxury status symbol

Purpose of page:
Show the types of conceptual Mars property locations you imagine.

Sections:

Olympus Mons View Zones
Underground Safety Habitats
Transport Hub Districts
Future Terraforming Regions
Luxury Interplanetary Estates

Again, use conceptual property wording unless you have legal ownership.

10. Investor Brief Page

Page name: Private Investor Brief
Main heading:
Mars Ascension Holdings

Use the CONFIDENTIAL INVESTMENT BRIEF section here.

This should not be your normal public page. It should be for serious people who want details.

Purpose of page:
More professional and private investor-facing page.

Use these sections:

The Next Great Wealth Transfer
Why This Matters
Early Positioning Advantages
The Psychology Of Historic Wealth
For A Select Few
Private Inquiries Only

Important:
Do not promise returns. Use words like:

speculative
visionary
long-term
conceptual
future-focused
high-risk
early-stage
11. Contact / Early Access Page

Page name: Contact / Early Access
Main heading:
Request Private Information

Use the email-style message here:

“Dear [Name], I hope this message finds you well…”

But make it shorter for the website.

Purpose of page:
Let people contact you.

Sections:

Name
Email
Phone
Country
Investment interest
Message

Call-to-action text:

The future belongs to those who position early. Request early-access information today.

12. Disclaimer Page

Page name: Disclaimer
Main heading:
Important Legal & Investment Notice

You need this page because your website talks about investment and Mars property.

What to say:

Mars Legacy Estates is a conceptual and future-focused project. Any reference to Mars property, interplanetary real estate, territorial access, or future development positioning is speculative and should not be understood as a guaranteed legal land ownership claim, guaranteed investment return, or affiliation with SpaceX, Tesla, Elon Musk, NASA, or any government agency unless officially stated.

This page is very important.

Best Website Menu Order

Use this order in your navigation:

Home
About Us
Our Vision
Why Mars
Life On Mars
Multiplanetary Future
Investment Opportunity
Future Property Concepts
Investor Brief
Contact
Disclaimer
Best Brand Name To Use

From your text, the cleanest name is:

Mars Legacy Estates

It sounds premium and clear.

Use Mars Ascension Holdings only for the investor/private side.

Example:

Mars Legacy Estates — public brand
Mars Ascension Holdings — private investor division

Final Advice

Your write-up is good, but it needs to be presented carefully. Make the website sound visionary, luxury, futuristic, and investment-focused, but avoid sounding like you are already legally selling real Mars land unless you truly have legal rights.

Best positioning:

A conceptual future-focused Mars property and investment vision inspired by humanity’s move toward multiplanetary civilization.
